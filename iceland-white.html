Frequently Asked Questions

What photography skill level is required?

Our tours are open to all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals. Our guides provide personalized instruction to help you improve your photography, regardless of your experience.

What equipment should I bring?

We recommend a DSLR or mirrorless camera with interchangeable lenses. A sturdy tripod is essential for long exposures, and a wide-angle lens is great for landscapes. Don't forget extra batteries, memory cards, and weather protection for your gear.

What is the expected physical fitness level?

Most locations are accessible with minimal hiking. However, some spots may require short walks on uneven terrain. A moderate level of fitness is recommended to fully enjoy the experience.
